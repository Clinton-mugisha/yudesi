Our Services

Our ServiceS

At Yudesi Primary School, we are committed to providing a holistic education that nurtures every child's academic, social, and personal growth. Located in the heart of Kampala, Uganda, our school offers a wide range of services designed to ensure excellence in all areas of development. Here's what sets us apart:

Consistent Success in UNEB Examinations

As one of Kampala's leading primary schools, Yudesi has a proven track record of excelling in the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) exams. Our students consistently rank among the best in the country, thanks to our focused preparation, rigorous assessments, and emphasis on foundational knowledge. We take pride in preparing our students to meet and exceed national standards

Comfortable Boarding Facilities

We offer a safe, secure, and nurturing boarding environment for students who require accommodation. Our boarding section provides a home away from home, ensuring students have access to nutritious meals, clean living spaces, and structured routines that support their academic and personal growth . Families can rest assured knowing their children are cared for by dedicated staff who prioritize their well-being.

Promotion of Talents Through MDD (Music, Dance, and Drama)

At Yudesi, we believe in nurturing creativity and self-expression. Our Music, Dance, and Drama (MDD) program allows students to explore their artistic talents and build confidence on stage. Whether it's performing at school events or participating in regional competitions, our students shine as they develop skills in performance, teamwork, and discipline

Academic Excellence and Top-of-the-Class Studies

Yudesi Primary School is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence. Our rigorous curriculum ensures students excel in core subjects while developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. With personalized attention and innovative teaching methods, we prepare students to consistently perform at the top of their class and achieve outstanding results in national exams

Dedicated and Highly Qualified Staff

Our team of passionate and experienced educators is the backbone of Yudesi Primary School. Every teacher is committed to fostering a love for learning and providing individualized support to help each student succeed. From classroom instruction to extracurricular guidance, our staff goes above and beyond to ensure students thrive academically and personally

Parental Engagement and Support

We value the partnership between parents and the school in shaping a child's future. Regular parent-teacher meetings, progress reports, and open communication channels ensure parents are actively involved in their child's education journey. Together, we create a supportive ecosystem for success

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Yudesi Primary School unique?

Yudesi Primary School stands out for its holistic approach to education, offering a balance of academic excellence, physical development through activities like swimming, and character-building programs such as Music, Dance, and Drama (MDD). Our boarding facilities provide a nurturing environment, ensuring students feel at home while developing essential life skills

Does Yudesi Primary School offer boarding facilities?

Yes, we provide comfortable and secure boarding facilities for students who require accommodation. Our boarding section ensures a structured routine with nutritious meals, clean living spaces, and 24/7 supervision by dedicated staff, creating a "home away from home" experience

What is the academic performance of Yudesi Primary School in national exams?

Yudesi Primary School has a proven track record of excelling in the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) exams. Our students consistently rank among the top performers in the country, thanks to our rigorous curriculum, personalized attention, and experienced teachers

How does the school ensure the safety of its students?

The safety of our students is our top priority. We maintain a secure campus with controlled access, provide safe transportation services, and ensure all staff members are trained in child protection protocols. Our boarding facilities also have round-the-clock supervision to ensure students feel safe and supported

Does Yudesi Primary School offer remedial classes for struggling students?

Yes, we offer free remedial and enrichment classes to ensure no student is left behind. These sessions provide additional support for students who need extra help, as well as advanced challenges for high achievers, ensuring everyone reaches their full potential
